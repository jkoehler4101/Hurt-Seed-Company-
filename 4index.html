Benefits of Hurt Seed Company Growing Production Soybeans

  • Wide maturity range for production(4.0-5.9).
  • Planting window from mid-May to mid-July.
  • Large, irrigated, double, double crop acres available for consistent quality.
  • All Hurt Seed Company production acres required to be sprayed with fungicide.
  • Large grower base, most with 30 plus years seed growing experience, with plentiful on-farm storage and hauling capabilities.
  • Centrally located to affordably ship to the north, Midwest and south to the Delta.
  • Large staff experienced in all facets of agriculture.
